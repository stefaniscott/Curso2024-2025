Application Requirements

Overview

The application will be built on top of the air quality dataset and will provide real-time access to air quality data across different locations, particularly focusing on port areas.

Key Requirements

  • The application should provide an interactive map showing air quality stations.
  • Users should be able to click on locations to view detailed air quality readings and trends over time.
  • There should be filters for different pollutants and monitoring timeframes (e.g., daily, weekly).
  • Integration with weather data for context.

Mock-ups

The application will feature:

  • A homepage with an interactive map interface displaying air quality stations.
  • Each station marker will open a window with detailed air quality metrics and historical data.
